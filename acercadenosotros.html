About Us

At MODEL, our vision is to promote young talents to bring out the best in them, regarding their image and education.

Since opening our doors in France 2001, MODEL has empowered women & men to build careers in the fashion world.

We use our platform to develop interesting new faces and grow careers for some of the world's most talented people in the pursuit of success.

Run by its owner & founder, Rafael, a young designer, MODEL is constantly innovating to connect creatives to top talent, including high fashion models and celebrities.
